The Grand Opening of Freepair Ireland!

Today is a truly magical day. Freepair Ireland opens it's doors to the masses, ready to spread the word about Right to Repair.

In these blogs, we will be covering news regarding the Right to Repair, all over the world, every week. Make sure to stay tuned!
